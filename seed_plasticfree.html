the fashion

lets make clothes!

sewing, knitting, crocheting. these are all skills i am slowly spending more time mastering. I can do simple sewn pants. knit small to medium pieces. crocheting is what i am the most inexperienced with, but i am hopeful i can assemble enough granny squares to make something wearable.

lets buy from small businesses!

everyone says that, then everyone else argues that no one could afford to only buy local/small. luckily i am privileged to not have any dependendents going through puberty or extreme fluctuations in my size. if i invest in 1-2 nice pieces a season (or a year) i can afford to splurge a little with someone local (or even the sale section of storymfg) and either keep for life or sell to get something new.

when making purchases, i've been compiling a lil notion of companies to support.
